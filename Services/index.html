Automation








RPA

RPA


Robotic Process Automation handles tedious, manual digital tasks and transfers the work of a human to a "digital worker". RPA solutions save companies time and money while enhancing productivity by allowing associates to focus on mission-critical tasks.



Chatbots

Chatbots


A chatbot is a computer program you can talk to through messaging apps, chat windows, or voice calling apps. These intelligent digital assistants resolve customer queries in a cost-effective, quick, and consistent manner.



Web Scraping

Web Scraping


Web scraping is the process of using bots to extract content and data from a website. The scraper can then replicate entire website content elsewhere.

For example: Periodically checks flight tickets and sends an alert in case of a price drop.



OCR

OCR


Optical Character Recognition is used for detecting printed or handwritten text characters inside digital images of paper files (PDF, email, etc).

For example: Get the invoice value from a PDF and update you excel table with it.

Excel / VBA

ERP Integration

ERP Integration


ERP integration connects and synchronizes ERP software to other applications and data sources.
It gives you a unified view of information from different systems in real time, whether the data originates in the ERP or other systems (for example, a website).



Web Development

Web Development


Developing a Web page involves designing and implementing the part that your readers see and interact with (front end), as well as the programming and database elements of the page (back end).
Maintenance includes hosting your page and updating it so that the content remains current.

KPI Reporting

KPI Reporting


KPI report makes it easy to see trends, recognize relationships or uncover outliers in their data. These insights lead to actions and better business results.
It helps businesses get more value from their data, promotes data literacy and creates a data-driven, decision-making culture.



Dashboards

Dashboards


The purpose of a dashboard is to get the information you are looking for at a glance. Therefore, data is shown in the form of graphs, and you can have quick indicators through colored keys, up or down arrows or highlighted figures, for example.
