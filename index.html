    About Us


    This story begins in 2016 when we went on a journey in a big multinational company. That's when we discovered that the average person spends half their day navigating multiple IT systems: filling out paper forms, waiting for their managers' approvals, etc...

    By getting familiar with Kaizen methodologies we figured out that, sometimes, companies don't need automation, they just need simplification and standardization of their services.

    With 10+ years of experience and a capable team of Business Analysts, Data Engineers and Developers, we are customer driven and will help you understand your needs, by finding solutions for problems that you didn't even know you have.

    We're data driven and will provide fully detailed reports of capital savings.
